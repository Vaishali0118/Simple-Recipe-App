Apple Pie

This apple pie recipe has been in my family for generation and never fails to delight.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 apples, peeled and sliced

Instruction

  1. pre-heat oven to 76oF(190oC)
  2. in a bowl mix sugar and cinnamon
  3. toss the apple in sugar cinnamon mixture
